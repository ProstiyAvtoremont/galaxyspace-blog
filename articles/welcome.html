Welcome to Digital Galaxy — Your Star Awaits

The universe is vast — and now a piece of it is yours. DOGE Galaxy lets you claim a unique star from a field of 2,000,000, name it, leave a message, and share it with the world forever.

How it works

Every star in the galaxy has a number from 0 to 1,999,999. When you purchase one, you choose a name and write a short message that gets attached to that star permanently. Anyone who visits the galaxy can hover over your star and read it.

Three tiers

Stars come in three tiers, each with a different visual style on the galaxy screen:

  • Basic ⭐ — $1 — a warm golden star, glowing softly in the field.
  • Supernova ⚡ — $2 — a bright red pulsing star, impossible to miss.
  • Constellation 🌌 — $4 — a cyan giant, the most prominent in the sky.

Payment

All purchases are made in DOGE — the currency of the stars. After completing payment, your star is activated automatically within seconds, and you receive a confirmation email with a direct link to your star.

Your link

Every star gets a permanent shareable link: galaxyspace.xyz/?star=YOUR_NUMBER. Send it to friends, post it on social media, or keep it as your own little corner of the digital cosmos.
